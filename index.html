Welcome to Wichita Carpet Cleaning Services

We are a proudly local business serving Wichita, Kansas and surrounding areas with exceptional carpet, tile, upholstery, and hardwood floor cleaning. Our team is committed to delivering fast, affordable, and professional results with a small-business level of care.

We specialize in using the Orbot SprayBorg™ system — a revolutionary, eco-friendly floor cleaning technology. Unlike traditional steam cleaners, the SprayBorg uses orbital agitation and controlled moisture to deep clean fibers while leaving carpets dry in about an hour. It's perfect for both residential homes and commercial properties, especially in high-traffic areas like offices, churches, and rental properties where downtime matters.

Whether you’re dealing with pet stains, high foot traffic, or just need a seasonal refresh, we have a cleaning solution tailored to your needs. Small details like deodorizing, enzyme treatment, and even free closet cleaning are included in many of our packages.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any added travel or service fees?

No, the price you see is the price you pay. No surprise fees—ever.

Do you move furniture?

We move small items (couches, loveseats), but not large sectionals or beds. Please move those before we arrive.

How long will the appointment take?

Most cleanings take 1.5 to 2 hours depending on the size and services.

What is the dry time?

1–2 hours for most carpets depending on material and environment.

Can I substitute a rug for a room?

Yes, as long as it’s standard size and not wool or too lightweight.

What payment forms do you accept?

Cash or credit card at time of service. We can also text a secure payment link.

Do I need to be home?

No, but we need access. Many clients provide garage codes or let us in via neighbors.

What if I have pet stains?

Choose the Pet Pro or Ultimate package—both include enzymes and deodorizing.

Do you clean Base Housing?

No, we currently do not service on-base military housing.
