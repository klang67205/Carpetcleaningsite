Frequently Asked Questions About Carpet Cleaning

Are there additional travel or service fees?

No, we do not charge additional fees. The price agreed upon during booking is the final invoiced amount after service.

Do you move furniture?

We move and return small furniture items, such as couches, loveseats, and small tables, to their original positions after cleaning. Larger items, like sectionals, entertainment centers, kitchen tables, dressers, or beds, are not moved. To clean under these items, please move them before our arrival.

How long does an appointment take?

Appointments typically last 1.5 to 2 hours, depending on the services ordered.

What is the drying time?

Drying time is approximately 1 to 2 hours, depending on carpet type and environmental conditions.

Can I substitute an area rug for a room in the 7-room package?

Yes, a standard-sized area rug can be substituted for a room, provided it is not too lightweight for our equipment. Wool rugs are excluded.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash and credit cards, payable at the time of service. If you‚Äôre not home, we can send a secure payment link via text for card payments.

Is it necessary to be home during the cleaning?

You need to provide access to the home and a way to lock up after we leave. We frequently clean vacant homes or properties where occupants let us in and leave, using methods like neighbor access, garage codes, or brief visits.

What do you recommend for pet stains and odors?

For pet-related issues, we recommend our Basic Plus or Premium Packages, which include specialized spot treatment and full-home deodorization.
